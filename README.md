# 📊 Marketing & Sales Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes a synthetic **Marketing & Sales Revenue Dataset** from Kaggle to evaluate marketing performance, sales trends, customer behavior, product categories, and regional performance.

The project combines SQL, Excel, Python, and Power BI to transform raw data into meaningful business insights and interactive dashboards that support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze marketing spending and sales revenue.
- Evaluate marketing efficiency using ROI.
- Identify the best-performing sales channels.
- Analyze product category performance.
- Discover customer segment behavior.
- Explore seasonal sales trends.
- Compare regional performance.
- Generate business recommendations based on data.

# 📈 Key Insights

- Revenue consistently peaks during **Q4**.
- Electronics generated the highest ROI.
- Home Appliances ranked second in profitability.
- Online sales generated the highest revenue.
- Wholesale achieved the highest ROI.
- Food & Beverage received the highest advertising spend but delivered relatively low ROI.
- Corporate and Regular customers contributed the largest share of revenue.
- Cairo recorded the highest sales revenue among all regions.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase investment in high-ROI sales channels.
- Optimize online advertising costs.
- Reevaluate spending on low-performing product categories.
- Plan marketing campaigns ahead of the Q4 peak season.
- Replicate successful regional strategies across other markets.
